Definitions of coat
n. - An outer garment fitting the upper part of the body; especially, such a garment worn by men. 2
n. - A petticoat. 2
n. - The habit or vesture of an order of men, indicating the order or office; cloth. 2
n. - An external covering like a garment, as fur, skin, wool, husk, or bark; as, the horses coats were sleek. 2
n. - A layer of any substance covering another; a cover; a tegument; as, the coats of the eye; the coats of an onion; a coat of tar or varnish. 2
n. - Same as Coat of arms. See below. 2
n. - A coat card. See below. 2
v. t. - To cover with a coat or outer garment. 2
v. t. - To cover with a layer of any substance; as, to coat a jar with tin foil; to coat a ceiling. 2
