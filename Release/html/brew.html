Definitions of brew
v. t. - To boil or seethe; to cook. 2
v. t. - To prepare, as beer or other liquor, from malt and hops, or from other materials, by steeping, boiling, and fermentation. 2
v. t. - To prepare by steeping and mingling; to concoct. 2
v. t. - To foment or prepare, as by brewing; to contrive; to plot; to concoct; to hatch; as, to brew mischief. 2
v. i. - To attend to the business, or go through the processes, of brewing or making beer. 2
v. i. - To be in a state of preparation; to be mixing, forming, or gathering; as, a storm brews in the west. 2
n. - The mixture formed by brewing; that which is brewed. 2
