Definitions of marc
n. - The refuse matter which remains after the pressure of fruit, particularly of grapes. 2
n. - A weight of various commodities, esp. of gold and silver, used in different European countries. In France and Holland it was equal to eight ounces. 2
n. - A coin formerly current in England and Scotland, equal to thirteen shillings and four pence. 2
n. - A German coin and money of account. See Mark. 2
