Definitions of dike
n. - A ditch; a channel for water made by digging. 2
n. - An embankment to prevent inundations; a levee. 2
n. - A wall of turf or stone. 2
n. - A wall-like mass of mineral matter, usually an intrusion of igneous rocks, filling up rents or fissures in the original strata. 2
v. t. - To surround or protect with a dike or dry bank; to secure with a bank. 2
v. t. - To drain by a dike or ditch. 2
v. i. - To work as a ditcher; to dig. 2
