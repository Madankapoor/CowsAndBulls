Definitions of left
imp. & p. p. - of Leave 2
imp. & p. p. - of Leave. 2
a. - Of or pertaining to that side of the body in man on which the muscular action of the limbs is usually weaker than on the other side; -- opposed to right, when used in reference to a part of the body; as, the left hand, or arm; the left ear. Also said of the corresponding side of the lower animals. 2
n. - That part of surrounding space toward which the left side of one's body is turned; as, the house is on the left when you face North. 2
n. - Those members of a legislative assembly (as in France) who are in the opposition; the advanced republicans and extreme radicals. They have their seats at the left-hand side of the presiding officer. See Center, and Right. 2
a. - Situated so that the left side of the body is toward it; as, the left side of a deliberative meeting is that to the left of the presiding officer; the left wing of an army is that to the left of the center to one facing an enemy. 2
