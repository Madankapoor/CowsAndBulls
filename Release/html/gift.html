Definitions of gift
v. t. - Anything given; anything voluntarily transferred by one person to another without compensation; a present; an offering. 2

v. t. - A bribe; anything given to corrupt. 2
v. t. - Some quality or endowment given to man by God; a preeminent and special talent or aptitude; power; faculty; as, the gift of wit; a gift for speaking. 2
v. t. - A voluntary transfer of real or personal property, without any consideration. It can be perfected only by deed, or in case of personal property, by an actual delivery of possession. 2
v. t. - To endow with some power or faculty. 2
