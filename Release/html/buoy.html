buoy

Definitions of buoy
n. - A float; esp. a floating object moored to the bottom, to mark a channel or to point out the position of something beneath the water, as an anchor, shoal, rock, etc. 2
v. t. - To keep from sinking in a fluid, as in water or air; to keep afloat; -- with up. 2
v. t. - To support or sustain; to preserve from sinking into ruin or despondency. 2
v. t. - To fix buoys to; to mark by a buoy or by buoys; as, to buoy an anchor; to buoy or buoy off a channel. 2
v. i. - To float; to rise like a buoy. 2
