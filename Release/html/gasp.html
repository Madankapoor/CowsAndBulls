Definitions of gasp
v. i. - To open the mouth wide in catching the breath, or in laborious respiration; to labor for breath; to respire convulsively; to pant violently. 2
v. i. - To pant with eagerness; to show vehement desire. 2
v. t. - To emit or utter with gasps; -- with forth, out, away, etc. 2
n. - The act of opening the mouth convulsively to catch the breath; a labored respiration; a painful catching of the breath. 2
