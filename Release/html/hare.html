Definitions of hare
v. t. - To excite; to tease, or worry; to harry. 2
n. - A rodent of the genus Lepus, having long hind legs, a short tail, and a divided upper lip. It is a timid animal, moves swiftly by leaps, and is remarkable for its fecundity. 2
n. - A small constellation situated south of and under the foot of Orion; Lepus. 2
