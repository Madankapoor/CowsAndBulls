Definitions of cake
n. - A small mass of dough baked; especially, a thin loaf from unleavened dough; as, an oatmeal cake; johnnycake. 2
n. - A sweetened composition of flour and other ingredients, leavened or unleavened, baked in a loaf or mass of any size or shape. 2
n. - A thin wafer-shaped mass of fried batter; a griddlecake or pancake; as buckwheat cakes. 2
n. - A mass of matter concreted, congealed, or molded into a solid mass of any form, esp. into a form rather flat than high; as, a cake of soap; an ague cake. 2
v. i. - To form into a cake, or mass. 2
v. i. - To concrete or consolidate into a hard mass, as dough in an oven; to coagulate. 2
v. i. - To cackle as a goose. 2
