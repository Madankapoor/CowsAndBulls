Definitions of lade
v. t. - To load; to put a burden or freight on or in; -- generally followed by that which receives the load, as the direct object. 2
v. t. - To throw in out. with a ladle or dipper; to dip; as, to lade water out of a tub, or into a cistern. 2
v. t. - To transfer (the molten glass) from the pot to the forming table. 2
v. t. - To draw water. 2
v. t. - To admit water by leakage, as a ship, etc. 2
n. - The mouth of a river. 2
n. - A passage for water; a ditch or drain. 2
