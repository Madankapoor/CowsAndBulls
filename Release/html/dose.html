Definitions of dose
n. - The quantity of medicine given, or prescribed to be taken, at one time. 2
n. - A sufficient quantity; a portion; as much as one can take, or as falls to one to receive. 2
n. - Anything nauseous that one is obliged to take; a disagreeable portion thrust upon one. 2
n. - To proportion properly (a medicine), with reference to the patient or the disease; to form into suitable doses. 2
n. - To give doses to; to medicine or physic to; to give potions to, constantly and without need. 2
n. - To give anything nauseous to. 2
