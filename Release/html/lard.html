lard

Definitions of lard
n. - Bacon; the flesh of swine. 2
n. - The fat of swine, esp. the internal fat of the abdomen; also, this fat melted and strained. 2
n. - To stuff with bacon; to dress or enrich with lard; esp., to insert lardons of bacon or pork in the surface of, before roasting; as, to lard poultry. 2
n. - To fatten; to enrich. 2
n. - To smear with lard or fat. 2
n. - To mix or garnish with something, as by way of improvement; to interlard. 2
v. i. - To grow fat. 2
