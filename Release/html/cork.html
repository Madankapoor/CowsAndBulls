Definitions of cork
n. - The outer layer of the bark of the cork tree (Quercus Suber), of which stoppers for bottles and casks are made. See Cutose. 2
n. - A stopper for a bottle or cask, cut out of cork. 2
n. - A mass of tabular cells formed in any kind of bark, in greater or less abundance. 2
v. t. - To stop with a cork, as a bottle. 2
v. t. - To furnish or fit with cork; to raise on cork. 2
