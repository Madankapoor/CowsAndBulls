Definitions of hour
n. - The twenty-fourth part of a day; sixty minutes. 2
n. - The time of the day, as expressed in hours and minutes, and indicated by a timepiece; as, what is the hour? At what hour shall we meet? 2
n. - Fixed or appointed time; conjuncture; a particular time or occasion; as, the hour of greatest peril; the man for the hour. 2
n. - Certain prayers to be repeated at stated times of the day, as matins and vespers. 2
