Definitions of dram
n. - A weight; in Apothecaries' weight, one eighth part of an ounce, or sixty grains; in Avoirdupois weight, one sixteenth part of an ounce, or 27.34375 grains. 2
n. - A minute quantity; a mite. 2
n. - As much spirituous liquor as is usually drunk at once; as, a dram of brandy; hence, a potation or potion; as, a dram of poison. 2
n. - A Persian daric. 2
v. i. & t. - To drink drams; to ply with drams. 2
