superl. - Moving with pain or difficulty on account of injury, defect, or temporary obstruction of a function; as, a lame leg, arm, or muscle. 2
superl. - To some degree disabled by reason of the imperfect action of a limb; crippled; as, a lame man. 2
superl. - Hence, hobbling; limping; inefficient; imperfect. 2
v. t. - To make lame. 2
