film

Definitions of film
n. - A thin skin; a pellicle; a membranous covering, causing opacity; hence, any thin, slight covering. 2
n. - A slender thread, as that of a cobweb. 2
v. t. - To cover with a thin skin or pellicle. 2
n. - The layer, usually of gelatin or collodion, containing the sensitive salts of photographic plates; also, the flexible sheet of celluloid or the like on which this layer is sometimes mounted. 2
