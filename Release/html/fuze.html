Definitions of fuze
n. - A tube, filled with combustible matter, for exploding a shell, etc. See Fuse, n. 2
- Alt. of Bickford match 2
n. - A wire, bar, or strip of fusible metal inserted for safety in an electric circuit. When the current increases beyond a certain safe strength, the metal melts, interrupting the circuit and thereby preventing possibility of damage. 2
