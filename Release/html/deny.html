Definitions of deny
v. t. - To declare not to be true; to gainsay; to contradict; -- opposed to affirm, allow, or admit. 2
v. t. - To refuse (to do something or to accept something); to reject; to decline; to renounce. 2
v. t. - To refuse to grant; to withhold; to refuse to gratify or yield to; as, to deny a request. 2
v. t. - To disclaim connection with, responsibility for, and the like; to refuse to acknowledge; to disown; to abjure; to disavow. 2
v. i. - To answer in /// negative; to declare an assertion not to be true. 2
